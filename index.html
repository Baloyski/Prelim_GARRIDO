About

SB19 was formed by ShowBT Philippines, the Philippine subsidiary of ShowBT Group in Korea.

SB19 is a Filipino boy band formed in 2016 by ShowBT Philippines. The group is composed of Pablo, Stell, Ken, Justin, and Josh. Their debut single "Tilaluha" was released on October 26, 2018, marking their official debut as a group.
